About us

TECHZONE is a chain of specialized hypermarkets for white and black goods, office equipment, and information technologies. The first TECHNOPOLIS opened doors in 2001, and today the company is already in charge of 33 hypermarkets in 25 large cities and towns all over Bulgaria. Three of the hypermarkets are situated in Sofia in the metropolitan quarters of MLADOST, LYULIN, NADEZHDA, Mall Serdika Center, The Mall, Bulgaria Mall and Paradice Center and the rest are placed in Plovdiv, Varna, Russe, Burgas, Stara Zagora, Pleven, Veliko Tarnovo, Vratza, Shumen, Sliven, Blagoevgrad, Dobritch, Yambol, Kyustendil, Pazardzhik, Kardzhali, Pernik, Silistra, Vidin, Montana, Razgrad, Haskovo, Gabrovo and Sandaski .

The consumers will find in TECHZONE over 25,000 appliances and accessories widely used at home, office and a car. Chain hypermarkets are upholstered and well-arranged with intention to offer comfort and convenience to the customers. There is easy access to the products and they are exhibited in such a way that the visitors can touch them, take by hand and see various appliances. In this way they can get acquainted with the product yet before purchase and might easier choose what they like most. TECHZONE brings together under one roof everything of the world of engineering, distributed in several main sectors.

There are brought together at one place appliances which make housekeeping easier and spare us time and efforts – refrigerators, cookers, dishwashers, and kitchen appliances designed to be built in, washing machines, dryers, air-conditioners, boilers. These are all technological achievements which are helping us take care of the house and keep it clean, comfortable and cozy.

In other sector of the hypermarket it is offered a huge variety of small electric appliances – coffee machines, grills, toasters, irons, free-turners, mixers, passators, microwave ovens, vacuum cleaners, etc. Here you can find a whole range of appliances for body care like hairdryers, hair-presses, clippers, epilators, etc.
