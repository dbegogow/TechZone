TECHZONE Extended Warranty

"TECHZONE Extended Warranty" is an insurance that provides you with continuous protection for your appliance after the expiration of the manufacturer's warranty. During the period, in which you are protected, your "TECHZONE Extended Warranty" pays for the repairs on your appliance, including the costs for labour and spare parts. If the manufacturer's warranty of the appliance you have selected is 2 years, you can purchase "TECHZONE Extended Warranty" for one or three additional years of extended warranty. If the manufacturer's warranty of the appliance is 3 years, the "Extended Warranty" insurance will provide you with two more years of protection for your appliance.

You can buy "TECHZONE Extended Warranty" from any hypermarket of the Technopolis chain. Please, contact one of our sales assistants to learn how to take advantage of this service.
